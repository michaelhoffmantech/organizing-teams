# Continuous Observability & Review Checklist

## Metrics Collection
- Automate backlog growth tracking
- Monitor deploy, defect, recovery, debug rates
- Track lead time & deployment frequency
- Collect role satisfaction data

## Review Cadence
- Monthly / Quarterly review of metrics
- Integrate observations into sprint retrospectives

## Team Engagement
- Discuss metrics in a trust-building manner
- Identify opportunities for team support or redesign

## Action Plan
- Adjust team structures based on observations
- Pilot improvements and measure impact
- Iterate continuously

# Enabling Collaboration & Growth

## Purpose
- Reduce friction across teams and systems.
- Promote smoother handoffs and predictable coordination.
- Enhance employee engagement through learning and visibility.
- Drive innovation via diverse collaboration.

## Practices
1. **Interaction Modes**
   - Define formal vs informal collaboration, frequency, and commitments.
2. **Roadmap & Priority Sharing**
   - Regular syncs (quarterly/monthly) for visibility.
   - Team API dashboards promote transparency.
3. **Paved Paths**
   - Standardized, supported approaches for reuse.
   - Reduces decision overload, promotes best practices.
4. **Career Growth & Upskilling**
   - Communities of practice, katas, and structured training.
   - Enable teams to learn and develop within their roles.

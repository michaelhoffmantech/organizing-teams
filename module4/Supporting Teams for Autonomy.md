# Supporting Teams for Autonomy

## Goals
- Teams execute independently with minimal handoffs.
- Progress measurable and tied to value.
- Team members feel ownership and accountability.
- Reduced management load for leaders.

## Practices
1. **Team API**
   - Defines ownership, support, and interaction rules.
   - Reduces dependency confusion and friction.
2. **Decision Guardrails**
   - Define when teams decide, collaborate, or escalate.
   - Reduces bottlenecks while managing risk.
3. **Dependency Reduction**
   - Encourage paved paths for standard, pre-approved solutions.
4. **Outcome-Oriented Backlogs**
   - Align work to goals and expected outcomes.
   - Ensure clarity on sprint objectives and deliverables.

**Step 1: Examine**
- Observe teams to identify misalignment or bottlenecks.
- Methods: Delivery stream mapping, metrics gathering.
- Outcome: Delivery flow map, ownership insights, baseline metrics.

**Step 2: Align**
- Align teams to value streams or domains.
- Methods: Reshape backlogs, assign ownership, pilot MVP scope.
- Outcome: Teams aligned to meaningful value with clear ownership.

**Step 3: Support**
- Support autonomy and clarity.
- Methods: Team API, decision guardrails, dependency reduction.
- Outcome: Independent execution, measurable outcomes, reduced friction.

**Step 4: Enable**
- Enable collaboration and growth.
- Methods: Interaction patterns, paved paths, career development.
- Outcome: Higher engagement, continuous learning, improved delivery flow.

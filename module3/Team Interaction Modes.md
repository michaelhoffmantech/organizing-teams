# Team Interaction Modes

| Interaction Mode    | Description                                      | Benefits                                            | Challenges / Considerations                          | Example Scenario |
|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|----------------|
| Collaboration       | Teams work closely together for a defined outcome | Accelerates discovery, blurs boundaries, builds shared understanding | Resource intensive, potential conflicts, requires decision framework | Orders & Customer teams collaborating on order validation system |
| X-as-a-Service      | One team consumes capabilities from another with minimal collaboration | Predictable delivery, clear ownership, reduces interruptions | Documentation gaps, risk of misalignment, potential innovation blind spots | Customer team creates developer portal for other teams to consume APIs |
| Facilitation        | Enabling team guides or upskills another team  | Reduces cognitive load, accelerates adoption, identifies gaps early | Justifying team existence, resource constraints, prioritization of focus | Developer Experience team providing cloud Paved Paths and workshops |

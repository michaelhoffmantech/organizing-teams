# RACI Chart Template for Team Communication

| Communication Task / Channel            | Manager | Team Lead | Core Engineer | Architect | TPM | SRE |
|----------------------------------------|--------|-----------|---------------|-----------|-----|-----|
| Scheduling meetings & agendas           | A      | R         | I             | I         | I   | I   |
| Meeting participation & discussion      | I      | A/R       | R             | R         | I   | I   |
| Knowledge sharing documentation         | C      | R         | R             | C         | I   | I   |
| Feedback & retrospectives               | A/R    | R         | R             | C         | I   | I   |
| Role introductions / relationship growth | R     | C         | I             | A         | I   | I   |
| AI-assisted meeting summaries           | I      | R         | R             | I         | I   | I   |
| Collaboration with other teams          | A      | R/C       | I             | C         | I   | I   |
| X-as-a-service / API engagement         | I      | C         | R             | C         | I   | I   |
| Facilitation / enablement sessions      | A/R    | C         | I             | C         | I   | I   |

**Legend:**  
- **R** = Responsible → Executes the task  
- **A** = Accountable → Owns the outcome  
- **C** = Consulted → Provides input  
- **I** = Informed → Kept up-to-date
